# BinMap Design System

## Design Philosophy

The platform should feel like:

- Google Maps
- Uber
- Linear

Not like:

- Government websites
- NGO websites
- Generic AI-generated websites

---

## Visual Style

Modern
Minimal
Map-focused
Professional

## Avoid

Do not use:

- Glassmorphism
- Excessive gradients
- Neon effects
- Parallax everywhere
- Heavy animations
- Overly green environmental themes
